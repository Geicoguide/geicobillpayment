# GEICO Bill Payment – Independent Insurance Billing Guide

This repository contains a professionally written HTML guide designed to help users
understand **GEICO bill payment** processes, including how to pay GEICO insurance bills,
manage billing cycles, avoid late fees, and resolve common payment issues.

## What This Project Is
- An **independent informational resource**
- A **billing and payment guidance website**
- Built with **clean HTML**, SEO-optimized structure, and accessibility in mind

## What This Project Is NOT
- Not affiliated with GEICO Insurance
- Does not process payments
- Does not provide official customer service

## Pages Included
- `index.html` – GEICO Bill Payment (main SEO page)
- `geico-bill-payment-help.html` – Ads-safe billing guidance page
- `insurance-billing-help.html` – Insurance billing hub (GEICO & Progressive)

## Key Topics Covered
- How to pay GEICO insurance bills online
- Billing cycles and due dates
- Automatic payments (autopay)
- Late payments and reinstatement
- Failed or delayed payment explanations
- General insurance billing guidance

## Technology Used
- HTML5 (no frameworks)
- Lightweight CSS
- No external images or scripts
- SEO-safe internal linking
